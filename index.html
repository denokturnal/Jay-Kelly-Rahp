About Jay Kelly Rahp

Jay Kelly Rahp, also known as Rapper Kpanaku, is a Ghanaian hip-hop artist and songwriter known for his unique blend of traditional Ghanaian sounds with contemporary hip-hop. Hailing from Accra, his music reflects the vibrant culture and rich musical heritage of Ghana while addressing social issues and personal experiences.

With a career spanning over a decade, Jay Kelly Rahp has established himself as a prominent figure in the African hip-hop scene. His lyrical prowess and dynamic performances have earned him a dedicated fanbase across the continent and beyond.

His latest single "Heavy Is The Head" showcases his evolution as an artist, blending thought-provoking lyrics with infectious beats that resonate with audiences worldwide.

Notable Achievements

  • Multiple award nominations in the Ghana Music Awards
  • Featured on major African music platforms and radio stations
  • Performed at major music festivals across Africa
  • Collaborations with top African artists
